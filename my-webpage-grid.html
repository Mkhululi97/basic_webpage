Hestorical Places in Ekurhuleni

Chris Hani Memorial

The memorial takes the form of a circle to symbolize unity, equality and inclusion. The five circles represent the five decades of the life shared so selflessly. The circular stairs encourage interactions, illustrating his warm personality, hospitality and philosophy of sharing

Hechter-Schulz Museum

The museum tells the stories, history, culture and traditions of the indigenous people of the African sub-continent and is extremely interesting. With a wide variety of artefacts that have been collected across the continent and displayed in a way that allows you to get a feel for their lifestyle. From beadwork to fossils, fertility dolls and musical instruments as well as a selection of clothing and items worn traditionally by the Xhosa, Zulu, Ndebele, Khoisan and Shangaan people as well as others

O.R. Tambo International Airport

It serves as the primary airport for domestic and international travel for South Africa. And since 2020, it is Africa's second busiest airport, In late 2005, a name change was proposed for the airport to "O. R. Tambo International", after former ANC President and anti-apartheid activist Oliver Reginald Tambo, an apparent change to the precedent of neutrally-named airports.
